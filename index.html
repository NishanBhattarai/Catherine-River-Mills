Catherine River Mills

Welcome to the official web site of the Catherine River Mills Apartments. Living at the Catherine River Mills is like living in the past but with all the modern amenities. The uniquely rehabilitated historical buildings consist of studio, one, two and three bedroom units with more than one hundred twenty different floor plans. The property is located on the banks of the beautiful Fox River. We invite you to continue on and explore the many different facets of the Historical Fox River Mills apartments and the area attractions that surround it.
